Golden Grain is a precision layout tool for woodworkers, framers, finish carpenters and designers who build to the golden ratio (φ ≈ 1.618).

Enter any dimension and instantly see it divided into two golden segments — the large segment A and the small segment B — in clean imperial inch fractions. Drive the calculation from whichever value you know: the combined length, the large segment, or the small segment, and the other two update live.

FEATURES

• Golden ratio engine — A = AB ÷ φ and B = AB − A, computed instantly.
• Inch-fraction output with feet roll-up, automatically reduced (e.g. 6 1/2").
• Type mixed measurements with a real keypad: whole numbers, fractions, feet and inches, and a SPACE key for values like 10 1/2.
• A live golden-ratio diagram with measurement graduations that match your selected precision.
• Decimal readout alongside every fraction.
